This type of bias alters the reader's opinion by describing an event only from one particular point of view or frame. There are two forms for framing bias:

  • Word choice (nouns or phrases: people, places, things, ideas, concepts). e.g. Both legal and illegal aliens will occupy 75 percent of new American jobs in as little as five years.
  • Labeling (adverbs or adjectives: describing nouns and pronouns). e.g. other Democrats visited a similar processing center in Clint, Texas, and made explosive allegations about the conditions there
